Our Benefits

We provide information on how malaria is transmitted from the bite of an infected mosquito(vector) and how to minimize the breeding areas.

Participating NGOs can provide mosquito nets and window screens, topical insect repellents and rapid diagnostic malaria tests and vaccines.

We conduct studies to investigate the malaria vectors breeding grounds in and around lake victoria.
